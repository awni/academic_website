Research

I hope to build programs which can learn to solve problems as well or better than humans. To that end, I research machine learning algorithms and applications. I am also interested in responsible machine learning. Below are some of the research areas I have worked or am working on:

  • Sequence Modeling (2012 - present)
  • Privacy Preserving Machine Learning (2018 - present)
  • Learning with Less Labelled Data (2018 - present)
  • Machine Learning in Healthcare (2016 - 2019)
  • Speech Recognition (2013 - present)
